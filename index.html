FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find fast answers to common questions about appointments, consultations, and our dental clinic policies.

How often should I visit a dentist?

We recommend scheduling a professional dental checkup and cleanings every 6 months to prevent plaque buildup, keep gums healthy, and diagnose any decay or issues early.

Is root canal treatment painful?

Not at all! With modern local anesthesia, specialized micro-tools, and experienced endodontists, a root canal treatment at SmileCare feels no different than getting a standard dental filling.

Do you provide emergency dental care?

Yes, we have dedicated emergency spots reserved daily for situations like severe dental trauma, knocked-out teeth, or sudden toothaches. Call our emergency hotline at +1 (555) 765-4321 for immediate guidance.

What payment options are available?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and work with multiple health insurance providers. For complex treatments, we also offer zero-interest clinical installment plans to match your monthly budget.
